


## User



Representation of a user.



---

***id*** [`UserId`](#userid)

Identifier of the user.



---

***memberOf*** [`OrganizationRef?`](/docs/organization--page#organizationref)

Organization Ref to which the user belongs.



---

***email*** `String`

Email address.



---

***givenName*** `String`

First name of the user.



---

***familyName*** `String`

Family name of the user.



---

***address*** `Address?`

Address of the user.



---

***phone*** `String?`

Telephone number of the user.



---

***roles*** `List<RoleIdentifier>`

Roles of the user.



---

***rolesComposites*** `RolesCompositesModel`

---

***attributes*** `Map<String, String>`

Platform-specific attributes assigned to the user



---

***enabled*** `Boolean`

Specifies if the user is enabled or not.



---

***disabledBy*** [`UserId?`](#userid)

Identifier of the user that disabled the user.



---

***creationDate*** `Long`

Creation date of the user, as UNIX timestamp in milliseconds.



---

***disabledDate*** `Long?`

Disabled date of the user, as UNIX timestamp in milliseconds.
