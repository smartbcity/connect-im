


### Query

Type: `UserPageQuery`

---

***organizationId*** [`OrganizationId?`](/docs/organization--page#organizationid)

Organization ID filter.



---

***search*** `String?`

Search string filtering on the email, firstname and lastname of the user.



---

***role*** `String?`

Role filter.



---

***attributes*** `Map<String, String>?`

Arbitrary attributes filter.



---

***withDisabled*** `Boolean`

If false, filter out the disabled users. (default: false)



---

***page*** `Int?`

Number of the page.



---

***size*** `Int?`

Size of the page.



---

***roles*** `List<String>?`

Roles filter.
