


## UserDTO



Representation of a user.



---

***id*** `String`

Id of the user.



---

***memberOf*** `OrganizationRefDTO?`

Organization to which the user belongs.



---

***email*** `String`

Email address.



---

***givenName*** `String`

First name of the user.



---

***familyName*** `String`

Family name of the user.



---

***address*** [`AddressDTO?`](/docs/commons-address--page#model)

Address of the user.



---

***phone*** `String?`

Phone number of the user.



---

***roles*** [`List<RoleDTO>`](/docs/api-privilege-role--page#roledto)

Roles assigned to the user.



---

***attributes*** `Map<String, String>`

Additional arbitrary attributes assigned to the user.



---

***enabled*** `Boolean`

Specifies if the user is enabled or not.



---

***disabledBy*** `String?`

Id of the user that disabled the user.



---

***creationDate*** `Long`

Creation date of the user, as UNIX timestamp in milliseconds.



---

***disabledDate*** `Long?`

Disabled date of the user, as UNIX timestamp in milliseconds.
