


# PermissionDTO



Allowed action within a system.



---

***id*** `PrivilegeId`

Generated id of the permission.



---

***type*** `String`

Type of privilege. See [PrivilegeType](/docs/api-privilege-role--page#privilegetype)



---

***identifier*** `PrivilegeIdentifier`

Identifier of the permission. Must be unique within a realm.



---

***description*** `String`

Description of the permission.
