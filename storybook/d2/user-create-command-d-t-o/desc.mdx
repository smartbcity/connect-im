


### Command

Type: `UserCreateCommandDTO`

---

***email*** `String`

Email address.



---

***password*** `String?` (default: null)

Password of the user.



---

***givenName*** `String`

First name of the user.



---

***familyName*** `String`

Family name of the user.



---

***address*** `Address?` (default: null)

Address of the user.



---

***phone*** `String?` (default: null)

Phone number of the user.



---

***roles*** `List<RoleIdentifier>`

Identifier of the [roles](/docs/api-privilege-role--page#roledto) to assign to the user.



---

***memberOf*** `String?`

Id of the organization to which the user belongs.



---

***attributes*** `Map<String, String>?` (default: null)

Additional arbitrary attributes assigned to the user.



---

***isEmailVerified*** `Boolean` (default: false)

False if the user has to verify their email.



---

***isPasswordTemporary*** `Boolean` (default: false)

True if the given password is temporary and has to be redefined on first login.



---

***sendResetPassword*** `Boolean` (default: false)

Send a reset_password email to the newly created user.



---

***sendVerifyEmail*** `Boolean` (default: true)

Send a verify_email email to the newly created user.
