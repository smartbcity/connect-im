


## RoleDTO



Named collection of permissions



---

***id*** `String`

Generated id of the role.



---

***type*** `String`

Type of privilege. See [PrivilegeType](#privilegetype)



---

***identifier*** `PrivilegeIdentifier`

Identifier of the role. Must be unique within a realm.



---

***description*** `String`

Description of the role.



---

***targets*** `List<String>`

List of entities the role can be applied to. See [RoleTarget](#roletarget)



---

***locale*** `Map<String, String>`

Human-readable translated names mapped by locale codes (e.g. "en", "fr", ...).



---

***bindings*** [`Map<String, List<RoleDTO>>`](#roledto)

Allowed sub-roles mapped per [target](#roletarget).



---

***permissions*** `List<PermissionIdentifier>`

Permissions granted to the role.
